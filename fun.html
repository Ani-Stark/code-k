Computer life hacks

These hacks will make your life easier, infact computer life easier.

Ask Google about any problem you’re facing.

You can type your problem into the Google search bar and read what various tech forums have to tell you. You will probably find the solution.

A laptop battery charges faster when in airplane mode.

Turns off Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Expect to see your laptop fully charged 10-15 minutes faster than if you had the airplane mode off.

Download Google image by pressing Alt and clicking on it.

Automatically downloads the image. Works only in google images,it will be saved on your computer in your Downloads folder.

Hide your IP address.

Hiding IP address means nobody sees what you’re doing after you hid your IP. You need to install a good VPN on your internet browser or VPN can be added externally

Clean and organize your PC

Untidy desktop is equivalent to your house. Clean your desktop and organizse it as you do in your home. This action will instantly maximize your computer's speed.

Play game

Don’t have an Internet connection? Play a dinosaur game on Google Chrome. Chrome displays a dinosaur on the broken page when not connected. Start by pressing Up arrow key and enjoy!

Tips and Facts

Below are some tips/tricks for coding dudes and non-coders will get some motivation after reading cool facts.

  • Use formatting tools

    It’s easy to write bad, unorganized code, but it’s hard to maintain such code. Formatter solves this.

  • Write Useful Comments

    Comments are very useful. You won't appreciate them until you leave your thousand line of code for couple of days, returning back will make your code senseless.

  • Use Meaningful Names

    Stop giving names like a, b, c etc.. Use proper names which signifies their roles.

  • Keep it as simple as you can

    Noob programmer write code that they understand. Pro coders write which gets understood by all.

  • Cut-paste > Copy-paste

    You will always regret if you cut and paste the code.
